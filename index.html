Relief from income tax, boost to PM Awas Yojana, hike in capital investment outlay - these were some of the key highlights in the Union Budget 2023-24 presented by Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman on Wednesday, 1 February. This was her fifth budget presentation and the final full budget by the Narendra Modi government before the crucial 2024 Lok Sabha elections.
